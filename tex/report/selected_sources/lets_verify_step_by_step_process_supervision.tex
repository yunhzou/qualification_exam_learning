\subsection{Let's Verify Step by Step: process supervision
\citep{lightman2023verify}}
\paragraph{Source type.} Research preprint.
\paragraph{Research question.} Ask whether supervising intermediate reasoning steps
produces more reliable mathematical reasoning than supervising only final answers.
\paragraph{Method.} Document the process-reward data collection procedure, outcome and
process reward models, best-of-$N$ selection, and the evaluation of individual steps.
\paragraph{Main evidence.} Record the comparisons between outcome and process supervision,
including which gains arise from reward-model training and which arise during search.
\paragraph{Connection to the position.} Supply the foundational distinction between
terminal outcome signals and localized process signals used by later agent critics.
\paragraph{Critical assessment.} Examine annotation subjectivity, mathematical-domain
specificity, step-boundary choices, reward-model calibration, and transfer to tool-using agents.
\paragraph{Takeaway.} Complete after reading.
