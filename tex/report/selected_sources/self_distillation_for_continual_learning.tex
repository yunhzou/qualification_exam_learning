\subsection{Self-distillation for continual learning
\citep{shenfeld2026selfdistillation}}
\paragraph{Source type.} Research preprint.
\paragraph{Research question.} Ask whether a model can acquire skills or knowledge from demonstrations while retaining prior capabilities through on-policy self-distillation.
\paragraph{Method.} Document the unconditioned student, demonstration-conditioned teacher, reverse-KL objective, teacher-weight choice, and sequential learning protocol.
\paragraph{Main evidence.} Record new-task accuracy, generalization beyond the training distribution, retention of prior capabilities, sequential-task results, and scale dependence.
\paragraph{Connection to the position.} Connect privileged-context teacher distributions to continual post-training and localized textual feedback in long agent trajectories.
\paragraph{Critical assessment.} Test the assumptions that in-context conditioning produces a near-optimal, minimally deviating teacher and that benchmark retention measures meaningful non-forgetting.
