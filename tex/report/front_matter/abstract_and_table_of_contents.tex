\begin{abstract}
This position paper studies how supervision and credit assignment shape large language
model post-training, from the Transformer architecture and outcome-reward reinforcement
learning to process critics, on-policy distillation, and continual self-distillation. The
working corpus deliberately includes peer-reviewed papers, preprints, technical reports,
and first-party research articles because recent production-scale post-training methods
are often documented first in technical publications. The central position, evidence,
qualifications, and conclusions will be developed as these sources are read in depth.
\end{abstract}
