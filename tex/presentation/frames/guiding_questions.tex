\begin{frame}{Guiding questions}
  Transformer foundations provide the architectural context for the post-training
  methods discussed here \citep{vaswani2017attention}.
  \begin{itemize}
    \item What behavior is each post-training method intended to change?
    \item What supervision, feedback, and optimization signals does it require?
    \item How convincing is the evidence, and what remains unresolved?
  \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
