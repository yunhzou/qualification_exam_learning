  \vspace{1mm}
  \footnotesize
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{The supervision moves:} OPSD distills the continuation; the proposal trains the hint tokens directly.
    \item At inference, the model emits its own hint and conditions later actions on it; false or mistimed hints therefore require gating.
  \end{itemize}
